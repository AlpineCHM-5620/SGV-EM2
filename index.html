What is Envy?

A simple definition:

Envy is sadness at another's good, a desire to see them lose good and for you to gain it.

How can it effect us emotionally?

Secular research has connected envy to bitterness, anger, depression, anxiety, and insomnia.

How can it effect us physically?

It has also been associated with physical health problems such as infections, cardio-vascular disease, and cancer. We are, quite literally, consumed by our envy

What has God told us about envy?

Proverbs 14:30 told us 3000 years before science did. We see a particularly vivid example of this in Jezebel who killed poor Naboth to get his vineyard, and was later eaten by dogs - 1 Kings 21; 2 Kings 9. Also see King Saul's jealousy of David 1 Samuel 18.

 Proverbs 14:30 says
“A heart at peace gives life to the body, but envy rots the bones.”
